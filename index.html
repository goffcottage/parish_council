Parish Council - Bourton on the Hill

Welcome to the Bourton on the Hill Parish Council website.

The Parish Council represent the parishioners in our village. We will drive local initiatives and where required communicate and lobby the District Council and the County Councils.

We look after various assets including the bus shelters, the defibrillators and the Millennium Wood. The Parish Council is consulted on planning matters, our views being passed on to the District Council. We also keep a look out for any highways related issues and liaise with the Highways department for their resolution.
